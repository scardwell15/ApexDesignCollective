Location: Hyperspace, fringes of Vela System
Date: 201.1.27

When the CDFS Harlan and the remnants of Patrol Squadron 17 came screaming though Vela's fringe jump point, Force Command reacted swiftly. Two Eidolon-class battlecruisers fresh out of fleet trials and a bevy of cruisers and heavy frigates reinforced the patrol, but with none of the Collective's critical "Paragon buster" Summit-classes available, the outcome of the fight would be far from certain.
